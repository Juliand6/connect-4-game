How to Play Connect 4

Connect 4 is a two-player game where players take turns dropping colored discs into a 7-column, 6-row grid. The objective is to connect four of your discs in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Players alternate turns, each dropping one piece per turn. The game ends when a player connects four discs in a row or when the grid is full. If no player wins, the game results in a draw.
